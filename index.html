By: Sean B.

When: The Great Hunt: Chapter 12

Rand is finding that his experiences are slowly becoming more and more out of his control. He's just finished checking the front gate as to wether or not his friends have arrived yet, but when he arrives back at his rooms he finds the building in flames and upon storming in to save the horn he finds that darkfriends have taken it elsewhere.

The problems he's dealing with are starting to go beyond what he bargained for. He is starting to realize that people can be hurt because of his resisting the Dark one, which is probably making him conside his options for running away to keep those he cares about from being hurt.

What a terrifying place to be all things considered. Honestly that would be the hardest part of being the hero, putting those you love in danger.
